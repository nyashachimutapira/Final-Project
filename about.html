Our Mission

At EventFinder, our mission is simple: to bridge the gap between event organizers and the community. We believe that local events are the heartbeat of a city, fostering connection, culture, and shared experiences. We provide a beautiful, simple, and elegant platform for discovering the amazing things happening right outside your door.

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a team of passionate developers and community advocates, EventFinder was born from a desire to solve a common problem: "What's there to do tonight?" We grew tired of scrolling through fragmented social media feeds and outdated websites. We envisioned a central hub for events—one that was as enjoyable to use as the events themselves were to attend. From that vision, EventFinder was built.

Meet the Founder

Nyasha Chimutapira, the visionary behind EventFinder, combines a deep love for technology with a passion for community building. With a background in web development and user experience design, Nyasha set out to create a platform that was not only functional but also a joy to use, ensuring that finding your next great experience is just a few clicks away.
